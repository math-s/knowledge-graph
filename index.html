How it works

From text to living tradition

FideMap turns a vast library of documents into a navigable web of ideas.

1

Every document is indexed

We ingest the full text of each Church document, preserving its structure, paragraphs, and citations.

2

Citations become connections

Each reference to another document, council, or Scripture passage is resolved into a live link in the graph.

3

You navigate freely

Start anywhere — a question, a concept, a specific text — and follow the tradition wherever it leads.
